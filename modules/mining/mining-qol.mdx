<Accordion title="Excavator" icon="sparkles">
  - **Excavator Solver**: Shows the solution for Excavator.
  - **Auto Excavator**: Automatically completes Excavator.
  - **Excavator Macro**: Keybind to start and stop the Excavator macro, reopens GUI and restarts Excavator.
  - **Excavator CPS**: CPS for Auto Excavator and Excavator Macro.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Refuel" icon="sparkles">

<Accordion title="Drill" icon="pickaxe" iconType="solid">
  - **Drill Lore Update Fix**: Fixes bug with drills lore update.
  - **Jasper Drill Exploit**: Requires Jasper Drill in hotbar, gives you +800 mining speed.

<Accordion title="ESP" icon="eye" iconType="solid">
  - **Fairy Grotto ESP**: Toggles Fairy Grotto ESP.
  - **Mines of Divan ESP**: Toggles Mines of Divan ESP.
  - **Precursor Remnant ESP**: Toggles Precursor Remnant ESP.
  - **Goblin Queen ESP**: Toggles Goblin Queen ESP.
  - **Goblin King ESP**: Toggles Goblin King ESP.
  - **Jungle Temple ESP**: Toggles Jungle Temple ESP.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Commissions" icon="sparkles">
  - **Auto Collect Commissions**: Automatically collects commissions.
  - **Auto Close GUI after collecting Commissions**: Automatically closes GUI after collecting commission.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Consumables" icon="sparkles">
  - **Auto Consume Consumables**: Toggles auto consumables.
  - **Auto Consume Pumpkin Powder**: Toggles auto consume Pumpkin Powder.
  - **Auto Consume Filet O' Fortune**: Toggles auto consume Filet O' Fortune.
  - **Auto Consume Chilled Pristine Potato**: Toggles auto consume Chilled Pristine Potato.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Lobby Switcher" icon="sparkles">
  - **Lobby Switcher:** Keybind to enable the lobby switcher. It switches lobbies until it finds one with a day equal to or less than the selected value.
  - **Max Day:** Maximum allowed day for the lobby.
</Accordion>
