---
title: "MuseumMacro"
description: "Automatically buys missing items for museum"
---

<Accordion title="General" icon="sparkles">
  - **Max price**: Maximum price for items to be donated. If the item is above this price, it will not be bought (<u>value multiplied by 1 million</u>).
  - **Sort by XP to Price Ratio instead of Price**: Sort items by XP to Price ratio when donating items

    <Info>
      You can enable & disable the macro in the Museum Menu by clicking button above it
    </Info>
  - **Disable**: Keybind to disable Museum Macro (works in GUI).
</Accordion>
