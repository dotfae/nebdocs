<Accordion title="General" icon="gear">
  - **Auto Accept**: Automatically starts minigames by clicking the NPC messages for you.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Zombie Shootout" icon="bow-arrow">
  - **Shoot Zombies**: Automatically completes Zombie Shootout minigame.
  - **Normal Zombie Speed**: Changes speed for normal Zombies.
  - **Small Zombie Speed**: Changes speed for Small Zombies.
  - **Rotation Speed**: General rotation speed.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Catch a Fish" icon="fishing-rod">
  - **Catch Fishes**: Automatically completes Catch a Fish minigame.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Beach Ball " icon="baseball">
  - **Beach Ball Macro**: Keybind to start and stop the Beach Ball macro.
  - **Reuse Beach Ball**: Automatically reuses Beach Balls.
  - **Use sus movement**: Suspicious movement to increase accuracy and rates.

    <Danger>
      This feature isn't bannable <u>for now</u>, but it might be in the future.
    </Danger>
</Accordion>
