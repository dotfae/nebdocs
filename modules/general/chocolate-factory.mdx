<Accordion title="General" icon="gear">
  - **Auto Upgrade Rabbits:** Upgrades rabbits in the best order automatically.
  - **Auto Activate Time Tower:** Activates time tower when it runs out.
  - **Auto Click Bonus Bunnies:** Collects bonus bunnies when they appear.
  - **Eggs ESP:** During Hoppity's Hunt, it will show all eggs to you.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Usage Notes" icon="note-sticky">
  Go into chocolate factory menu and afk.
</Accordion>
