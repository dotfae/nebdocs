<Accordion title="General" icon="gears" iconType="duotone">
  - **Diana Macro Keybind**: Keybind to start and stop the macro.
  - **Alert on Inquisitor Spawn**: Sends you a windows alert when an Inquisitor spawns.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Weapon" icon="swords" iconType="duotone">
  - **Weapon Type**:
    - **Melee**: Kills mobs using a sword.
    - **Ability/Bow**: Kills mobs using your bow or mage weapon ability.
    - **Hyperion**: Uses Wither Impact when in range.
  - **Weapon Slot**: Configurable weapon slot (1-8) for your hotbar.
  - **Travel Mode (BETA)**:
    - **Walk and Warp**: Walks and uses island warps to travel.
    - **AOTV**: Uses Aspect of the Void to travel.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Warp" icon="galaxy" iconType="duotone">
  All these warps will be used if checked and available.

  - **Warp to Castle**
  - **Warp to Crypt**
  - **Warp to Dark Auction**
  - **Warp to Museum**
  - **Warp to Stonks**
</Accordion>
